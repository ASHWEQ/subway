Click a Stop!

NYC Travel Time Map

When you click a stop, the map adjusts so that every other stop‘s distance is proportional to travel time.

Your can use the trackpad or scroll wheel to zoom, and drag to move around the map.

Travel time predcictions are based on the MTA‘s published schedule, assuming an 8 AM weekday departure. They may be a little optimistic these days.
